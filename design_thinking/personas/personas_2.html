Persona Creation Guidelines

• Keep personas to one page, so they remain effective communication tools and can be referred to quickly during design discussions.

• Add personal details but don’t go overboard.

• Include goals for each persona. This can include experience goals as well as end goals. For example, Bob the expert mechanic, an experience goal would be to ‘look knowledgeable’, while an end goal would be ‘remain informed about the company’.

Source: An Introduction to Personas and How to Create Them
